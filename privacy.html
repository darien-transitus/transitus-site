Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 12, 2026

Transitus, LLC ("Transitus," "we," "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

Information we collect

When you fill out a form on our website, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your phone number
  • Your email address
  • The property address you're inquiring about

We may also automatically collect basic technical information when you visit our website, including your IP address, browser type, device type, pages visited, and approximate location. This is used to understand how visitors use the site and improve it.

How we use your information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to your inquiry about your property
  • Prepare your Transitus Options Sheet
  • Communicate with you about your specific situation
  • Refer you to relevant vendors (general contractors, attorneys, housing counselors, etc.) only with your consent

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not share your information with anyone outside of Transitus and its directly retained vendors without your explicit consent.

Cookies and analytics

Our website uses cookies and analytics tools (including Google Analytics and Google Ads) to understand site usage and measure marketing effectiveness. These tools may collect anonymized data about your visit. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

How we store and protect your information

Information submitted through forms on our website is transmitted via secure HTTPS and stored with our form processing service (Formspree). We retain submitted information only as long as needed to communicate with you and serve your inquiry.

Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to information we hold about you
  • Request that we delete information about you
  • Withdraw your consent for us to contact you at any time

To make any of these requests, email us at darien@gettransitus.ai.

Children

Our services are intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18.

Changes to this policy

If we update this policy, we'll update the "Last updated" date at the top. Material changes will be communicated to current Transitus clients directly.
